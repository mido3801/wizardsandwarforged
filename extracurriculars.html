Strixhaven Extracurriculars in Eberron

Each player is allowed to choose 2 extracurriculars to benefit from. If a player wishes to change extracurriculars, they may do so, but will need to wait a week of in-game time before they can gain the new benefit.

Not Quite Fluent: You can get the gist of conversations and writing that take place in languages you don't know, though you don't know enough to respond.

Faculty Advisor: Leren/Laren Lanner

Ink to Life: You can sketch a relatively simple object and then, as an action, make it real for 1 minute. The object can't have any magical properties and disappears after the duration.

Faculty Advisor: Ari Isti

En Passant: When an enemy moves through a square adjacent to you, you can use your reaction to move to the square they just vacated. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.

Faculty Advisor:Maia-zak

Archivist's Insight: After studying a text or document for an hour, you can determine its age, origin, and if it has been tampered with or altered in any way.

Viral Marketing: Whenever you're trying to spread information or rumors, you can do so with remarkable speed and reach. Within an in-game day, your message reaches twice as many people as it would under normal circumstances.

Kinesthetic Awareness: You always know the direction of "up" and "down," even in conditions like zero-gravity or underwater, and have advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone.
