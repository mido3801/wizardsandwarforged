Prismari

This college is where elemental magic meets artistic flair. Students learn to channel their creativity into elemental expressions, whether that's fire sculptures or watercolor spells. Many students become entertainers, showcasing their magical talents in performances.

Quandrix

This arcane school explores the perplexing realms of mathematical realities and dimensional magic. Its students, often mentored by archfey or researchers from House Cannith, seek to understand the multi-dimensional facets of the universe.

Witherbloom

This college focuses on the symbiotic relationship between life and death. Students are taught to harness the energies of nature to both heal and harm. A balanced approach is emphasized, leading many to become proficient in both druidic and necromantic magic.

Lorehold

Centered on the study of history and time, this college has artificers and wizards keen on understanding the passage of time and historical events. Many delve into the intricacies of the Draconic Prophecy, attempting to decode its impact on past, present, and future.
