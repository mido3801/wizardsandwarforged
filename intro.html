Strixhaven: Legacy of Eberron

In the heart of Eberron, where the arcane energies pulsate with life, lies the iconic Strixhaven University. Built 300 years ago by the grandeur and foresight of the mighty dragons, this institution was conceived as a beacon of enlightenment, drawing magic wielders from every corner of the continent. Strixhaven was more than just a place of learning; it was a symbol of unity and progress, a testament to what the races of Eberron could achieve when magic became the bond that ties.

But times have changed. The very essence of Strixhaven has been tested. The scars of the Last War, a conflict that ravaged the lands and souls of Eberron, have left a deep imprint on the university. Where once the halls echoed with the laughter of students from diverse backgrounds, now there is a palpable tension. The alliances formed within these walls are fragile, and the threat of dissolution looms large.

To counteract the wave of doubt and to preserve the sanctity of the institution, the council of Strixhaven has taken a drastic step. This year, admittance is exclusive to wizards alone. It's a move that has raised eyebrows and stirred debates across Eberron. Some see it as a desperate attempt to regain the lost glory, while others believe it's the only way to preserve the purity of arcane arts.

As a new class of wizards, you stand on the precipice of a world that's on the cusp of transformation. Your journey at Strixhaven will be unlike any other. The choices you make, the alliances you form, and the magic you wield will not only shape your destiny but also the fate of Strixhaven and, perhaps, all of Eberron.

Prepare yourselves, for the challenges ahead are formidable. But remember, in the hallowed halls of Strixhaven, magic isn't just a tool; it's a way of life. Will you rise to the occasion and solidify the legacy of this institution, or will you be a witness to its final chapter?
